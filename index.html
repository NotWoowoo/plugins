Digital Distortion

This deceptively simple single-knobed distortion plugin folds the wave onto itself to draw out noisy, metalic timbres. I would recommend using it for adding some interesting high-end to sounds, but the possibilities are really endless.

*dry-wet knob not included

Honeycomb

This is a comb filter, but not quite. Modulate the delay time to hear a very weird effect. This weird viscous effect is caused by the delay time smoothly interpolating from one value to the next, making the input sound interact with itself in strange ways.

Learn More

Infinigain

Have you ever wanted to turn up your signal by 60+ dB? No? Well you'll find how useful it is to do so when a snippet of audio is too quiet to turn up without using a billion gain plugins. You can also use it for niche sound design purposes (try some insane compression on a quiet or inaudible sound with this and a limiter after it).
